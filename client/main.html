Looks & works identical to facebook comments

Using a fomment section in your landing page means giving each customer the ability to comment, like, report, reply, delete, edit, and view facebook profiles of users just like real facebook comments. This is all without actually being connected to facebook, and without letting customers see each other's comments. You choose and configure which initial comments will load, and choose how many likes each comment starts with. Fomments stores customer comments in the browser so even on refresh, customers will see the saved state of their comments.
